Sale only available products on Website

Customers cannot order more products than available in stock

Usage

After installing the module go to Website → Shop and, for example, add to cart some kind of products.

In the section "My Cart" that appears you can see the "Available" column is added. This column shows the total quantity of products available in stock.

Once quantity of products will be greater than available in stock, the corresponding value in "Quantity" column will turn red and the "Process checkout" button will become inactive.

Additional useful function of the module is that a customer can be redirected back to My Cart after clicking "Confirm" in the "Shipping & Billing" tab. This happens if some ordered products had been removed from stock, while he was typing his address.

For example, suppose we have two customers: Customer1 and Customer2. Assume, Customer1 has added a maximum quantity of some available product to cart and he has passed all the steps Review Order ⇒ Shipping & Billing ⇒ Payment ⇒ Confirmation. In this case, Administrator of the Website will receive quotation, where he can confirm sale or cancel it.

Suppose now that, at the same time, Customer2 orders some quantity of the same product as Customer1 and then moves on to the 2nd step (Shipping & Billing) to fill his address. Once Administrator clicks the "Confirm Sale" for Customer1's order, Customer2 will not be able to move to the next step, and even the contrary, he will be redirected back to cart after clicking the "Confirm" button.
